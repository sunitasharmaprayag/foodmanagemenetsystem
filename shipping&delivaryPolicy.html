Shipping Policy

Our website tries its best to provide a cost effective shipping to our customers and the product comes in a good packaging. Provides a fast delivery to customers.

The order received is processed in 48 hours. Our logistics partners service is great and assure a fast delivery of product.

Get free shipping on orders of Rs 500 and above anywhere in India. Flat Rs. 100 delivery charge for orders less than Rs. 500. The estimated time of shipment delivery is 4-5 days

Customer Care-

The services offered by us cancellation, refund any queries are attended by us are attended in minimum 2 days. We are available 6 days in a week From Monday to Saturday (9.00 to 20.00 IST).

Please note that our shipping policy is applicable within India and outside India (International Couriers) too.

Kindly Note Our Policies Below –

A complete postal address including pin code, email id and contact number is essential to help us ship your order. Kindly cross-check your pin-code and contact number before you complete your order.

f the ordered item is in stock, it will be packed and dispatched from our warehouse within 3 working days.

However if some of the ordered items are not in stock, then we will get them produced and have them dispatched within 10 working days of the order being placed. We will keep you informed under such circumstances.

Delivery time is subject to factors beyond our control including unexpected travel delays from our courier partners and transporters due to weather conditions and strikes.

As soon as your package is dispatched, we will email you your order tracking details. Kindly bear with us until then.
